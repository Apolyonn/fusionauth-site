Build multi-step custom registration forms via API or the administrative user interface.

START FREE TRIAL

Add custom form fields

Use standard form fields like email, name, and password, or create your own. Custom fields can be strings, numbers, dates or booleans. Validate the fields to ensure no dirty data gets into your user database.

Create multi-step forms

Create multi-page registration forms, providing contextual instructions and collecting different information on each page. Don't overwhelm your users during the sign up process.

Theme the form

Make the form your own. Use FusionAuth's rich theming functionality to give your registration a custom look and feel and add error messages. Localize the form to reach all your audiences.
